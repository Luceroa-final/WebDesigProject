Going to a concert, i’ts all about finding a vibe, finding a place where you belong, If you overthink, YOU WON’T ENJOY!

People won’t bite you in a concert, unless you get inside a moshing or slam dancing

Best way to enjoy a concert it’s dancing, like if you don’t care about anything. Specially because you are by yourself.
4- Beer might be your friend
If you are shy or afraid to talk to other people, beer might help you.
6- Be Present
Going Alone to a concert might be something new for you, sometime s because of nerves you might forget to be present an enjoy. So don’t forget to be present.
7- Try to get to the front
The best thing about going to a concert alone, it’s that you don’t have to worry about your peers, so be fearless and find your way to the front.
8- If you get nervous, crows are good to hide.
If for ever reason, you get nervous or just overwhelmed and don’t not what to do, remember that in crows you are invisible.
9- Keep yourself hydrated
Concerts are amazing, but can get a bit sweaty so don’t forget to drink a lot of water before and after the concert.

A Concert is the place to be yourself! Do whatever you want!.
